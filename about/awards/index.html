Engineering Society Awards

\r\n

Awards are given annually to deserving students by the University of Toronto Engineering Society. These awards\r\n generally recognize contributions by students to the Engineering Society, either through their position as a\r\n representative, or through a project they have managed over the course of the SkuleTM year. Details\r\n regarding awards can be found in the Policy on Awarding Society Awards, found under Governing Documents.
