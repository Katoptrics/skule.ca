\n University of Toronto Engineering Society\n

\n \n

\n \n \n \n Founded in 1885, we are the student government for Skule\xe2\x84\xa2, the student body at the University of Toronto\xe2\x80\x99s Faculty of Applied Science and Engineering. We provide services, funding, and countless extracurricular opportunities to over 4500 full-time members, proudly continuing the traditions and storied history of Canada\xe2\x80\x99s oldest and largest engineering school.\n

Finances

\r\n

The Engineering Society helps with financing student projects. You can request funding for Special Projects, receive Cheque Requisitions for past expenses, and apply for Levies from Student fees.

Bookings & Rentals

\r\n

The Engineering Society provides room bookings on campus and a parking spot booking for EngSoc-affiliated clubs and project directorships, as well as rentals of audio/visual equipment. Additionally, EngSoc rents out lockers for students in certain Engineering buildings.
