Emergencies

\r\n

For any critical emergency, please call 911. If it is specific to the University of Toronto, consider calling Campus Police at \r\n 416-978-2222 or visit their website. Additionally, you can sign up to receive \r\n emergency alerts at alert.utoronto.ca.
