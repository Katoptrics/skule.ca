Finance Committee

Founded in 2008, following a call for increased accountability and help with a high volume of funding applications and allocations, the University of Toronto Engineering Society Finance Committee is a standing committee of the Engineering Society Board of Directors. As a standing committee, whose term lasts 1 year, members of the FC reserve no particular authority but are tasked with helping shape the Societyâ5™s financial future. Basically, they exist to provide the Board with funding recommendations and assist clubs who seek financial help or guidance.

For the 2021-2022 year, it will be comprised of 2 Officers (VP Student Life and VP Finance), 7 members-at-large and 1 members of the Board of Directors. The following document provides a short introduction to the Engineering Society Finance Committee for those unfamiliar with their work. For more information, please refer to this.
